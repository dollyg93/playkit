This Playkit contains 14 carefully designed fun and progressively challenging activities to ensure children develop of a full range of movement skills, in terms of their stability, locomotion and object control. It helps coaches create an activity for children and assess their movement in a simple way that engages children along the way. Coaches can use the Playkit in any sport program. Becoming more competent in performing these movement skills in increasingly challenging games situations will prepare children for participation in sport specific, dynamic environments.
