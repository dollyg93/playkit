WHAT IS THE PLAYKIT?

The Playkit is a games-based resource designed to improve the movement skills of children between the ages of 8 and 12 years in a fun and dynamic way.

Development of movement skills during childhood is crucial to enable participation in a wide range of sports and activities at any time of life.
