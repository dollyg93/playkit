ASSESSMENT ACCURACY TRAINING

HOW TO USE THIS PLAYKIT

This Playkit is designed for coaches to provide fun and progressively challenging activities for children aged 8-12 years and ensure that children develop a full range of movement skills, in terms of their stability, locomotion and object control. It helps coaches create an activity for children and assess their movement in a simple way that engages children along the way.
